01 / About

A three-day suspension retreat in the cloud forest above Teocelo, Veracruz.

Xalapa Zipline Suspensions gathers a small group of practitioners and participants of all experience levels for several days of suspensions and rest in the mountains of central Veracruz.

01 Jungle retreat atmosphere A forested mountainous site surrounded by Veracruz cloud forest.
02 Community-oriented Shared meals, shared rooms, shared rigging — built around presence.
03 Zipline suspensions Hook suspensions performed along a rigged zipline through the canopy.
04 Nature immersion Open daylight rigging, evening fire, full retreat from city noise.
05 Shared meals & lodging Three meals daily, cabin-style sleeping arrangements onsite.
06 Temazcal ceremony Traditional pre-Hispanic sweat lodge ceremony held in the evening of Nov 10.
07 Intentional practice A respectful, careful, ceremonial approach to suspension.
08 All experience levels Open to seasoned practitioners and first-timers alike, with guidance throughout.
Note · Location

Although the event is called Xalapa Zipline Suspensions, the actual retreat location is in the nearby village of Teocelo, Veracruz — about 45 minutes from Xalapa.

03 / Included

What your registration covers — and what it doesn't.

Included In registration

  • ✓ Shuttle transportation. Round-trip from Abracadabra Tattoos & Piercings in Xalapa to the retreat site in Teocelo.
  • ✓ Lodging. The nights of November 9, 10, and 11 in shared cabin accommodations.
  • ✓ Three meals daily. Breakfast, lunch, and dinner served throughout the retreat.
  • ✓ One guaranteed suspension. Each attendee receives one guaranteed suspension opportunity.
  • ✓ Core retreat activities. Zipline suspensions, jungle suspensions, and open daytime activities.

Not Included Extra / not in registration

  • — Personal travel expenses. Meals, transit, and incidentals outside the retreat dates.
  • — Personal purchases. Anything bought outside the included program.

06 / Travel

From international airport → Xalapa → Finca Santa Rosa.

Step 01

Getting to Mexico

Most international attendees fly into Mexico City (MEX). Some attendees may fly into Veracruz directly. Mexico City is usually less expensive and offers more international connections.

Main hubMEX
Alt hubVER
Step 02

Getting to Xalapa

Most attendees take an ADO bus from Mexico City to Xalapa. The bus ride is approximately 4 hours. ADO buses are comfortable, modern, and commonly used for long-distance travel in Mexico. Book tickets via the ADO app or website.

Duration~4 hrs
BookingADO app / web
Step 03

Shuttle to retreat

Shuttle transportation from Xalapa to the retreat is included. Departure point:  Abracadabra Tattoos & Piercings, Xalapa, Veracruz. Shuttle departs the morning of November 9.

DepartsNov 09 · 08:00
ReturnsNov 12
